Insights

This section analyzes the trends in public transport complaints over the years, focusing on identifying key growth patterns and areas where concerns are concentrated. By looking at fluctuations in complaint numbers, we can determine the specific issues causing dissatisfaction, such as delays, overcrowding, or inadequate services. Understanding these patterns helps highlight where improvements in service, communication, or infrastructure might be needed to address customer concerns and enhance overall service quality.

Future Directions

This section examines the potential future of public transport services by using historical complaint data to anticipate emerging trends. The analysis highlights which areas of public transport are likely to need further attention, such as infrastructure upgrades or service expansions. By addressing recurring issues, policymakers and service providers can plan targeted strategies for improvement. This approach aims to ensure the sustainable development of public transport services, with a focus on better user experience and operational efficiency in the long term.
